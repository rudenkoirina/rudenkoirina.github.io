Background

I earned my Ph.D. in psychology at the Moscow State University in 1988, researching interpersonal interaction styles and their determinants. In the following years I worked in academia on various psychology problems, wrote for a popular psychology journal on topics related to children, parents and education, worked with children in rehabilitation and development centers, and in a private school as a school psychologist. Furthermore, I have several years of corporate experience in Human Resources, in career development and education guidence, and in private consulting.
